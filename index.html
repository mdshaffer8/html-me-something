2017 Year in Review

Camping at Hog Island

February

Took a weekend camping trip with by boyfriend, Sam, to Hog Island. We spent two nights Tent Camping, which is one of our favorite things to do! We canoed down the Withlacoochee River and took a break to eat some smoked mullet that we had bought from a roadside fish stand.

Trip to Everglades City

March

For Sam's Birthday we took a trip to Everglades City. It's the coolest small town I've ever been to. We stayed at the Rod and Gun Club, which is a historic lodge built in the 1890s. My favorite things to do here were visiting the Big Cypress Bend Boardwalk, taking an Airboat Tour through the mangroves, and taking a day road trip through Old Loop Road.

Puerto Rico Trip

April

Sam and I took a trip to Puerto Rico to celebrate a friend's birthday. Sam lived here before when he was in the Coast Guard, so he knew all the great spots to go to! We arrived early so we stayed the first night in Utuado, which is in the mountains. For the rest of the trip, we stayed in Isabela. We also got a chance to take a day trip to Old San Juan since our flight was delayed by several hours.

Ohio Trip

May

My son, Kai, and I went to Ohio to visit my oldest sister Shannon. All of the sisters (there are a total of 5 of us) met to have a weekend vacation together. Pictured here is Kai (with the glasses) and my nephew, Jack.

RIP Scott Shaffer

June

My father died on June 10th. He had only just been diagnosed with Stage IV Lung Cancer a month prior, on May 5th.

Trip to Orlando

July

I took a short weekend trip to Orlando with Erica (sister), Dallas (niece), and Kai. It was a quick trip to just relax and get our mind off of things for a moment. These are the times that are most special to me.
